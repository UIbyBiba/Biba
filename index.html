Good questions

Things founders ask us.

How fast can you deliver? +

Most websites take around two weeks and app designs around three to four, depending on scope. Speedy delivery is our thing — we'll give you a firm timeline before we start, and we hit it.

I've been burned by a freelancer before. Why is this different? +

Completely fair to be cautious. We keep communication clear from day one, share progress regularly, and hand over finished, launch-ready work. No ghosting, no surprises — just a designer who shows up.

Do you work with founders outside South Korea? +

Yes — we're based in Jeju and work with founders all over the world, fully online. Time zones are no problem; we'll find a rhythm that works for you.

How does pricing work? +

Simple, project-based pricing — you'll know the full cost before we begin, with no hourly meter ticking. Book a call and we'll give you a clear quote for exactly what you need.
