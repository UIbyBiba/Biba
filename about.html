About

The designer founders can count on.

Studio Jeju is a small design studio with a simple goal: to be the reliable, fast, genuinely good designer that founders wish they'd found the first time.

Our story

Why Studio Jeju exists.

Too many founders have the same story: they hired a freelancer, waited weeks, chased for updates — and ended up with work they couldn't even launch. We started Studio Jeju to be the opposite of that.

For 2+ years we've designed premium mobile apps and websites for startups and small businesses, with a focus on two things most freelancers get wrong: showing up and shipping on time.

We're based on Jeju Island in South Korea and work with founders all over the world. Small studio, high standards, and a real person who answers — that's the whole idea.
