# Customer Profile — Studio Jeju

_Built from your business profile. This is who every page should speak to._

## Who they are: "The Founder"

A startup founder or small-business owner — often non-technical, wearing many hats. They
have an idea (or a growing business) and need a professional app or website to launch it or
make it look legit. They work with people online and are comfortable hiring remotely. They're
not chasing the cheapest option; they want it done **right** and **fast**.

## What they're really trying to do

- **The task:** Get a polished app or website designed and shipped so they can launch or grow.
- **How they want to feel:** Confident it'll look professional — and relieved to finally have
  someone reliable who actually delivers.
- **How they want to look:** Credible and legit to their own users, customers, and investors.

## What pushes them to look for you (trigger moments)

- They're about to launch and need it to look the part.
- A previous freelancer ghosted them, blew the deadline, or delivered sloppy work.
- Their current site/app looks amateur and is costing them credibility.
- A deadline is coming — a demo, an investor meeting, a launch date.

## What worries them (the objections your site must answer)

1. **"Will they disappear on me like the last one?"** → reliability
2. **"Will it actually be done on time?"** → speedy delivery (your edge)
3. **"Will it look professional and premium?"** → proof of quality work
4. **"Is the price fair?"** → premium positioning, clear value

## What makes them choose Studio Jeju

- **Visible proof** — real screenshots of finished, high-quality work.
- **Speed they can count on** — your promise of fast delivery, stated plainly.
- **A premium-but-friendly feel** — looks high-end, sounds human and trustworthy.
- **An easy next step** — one obvious button: *book a call*.

## The words they use (good for the site + Google later)

- "I need an app" / "I need a website"
- "launch," "get it live," "MVP"
- "my designer ghosted me," "blew the deadline"
- "make it look professional / clean / legit"
- "fast turnaround," "quick"
